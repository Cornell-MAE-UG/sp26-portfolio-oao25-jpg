Problem: Draw a diagram of a nutcracker that can crack a macadamia nut specifically

The constraints on this design are based on values that I will give below

For this project, certain parameters were needed to create a design that was both functional and practical. The following information was used:
Force to crack a macadamia nut: 241 kg x 9.8 N = 2370 N
Average diameter of a macadamia nut = 20 mm
Average grip strength = 606.8 N < x < 961.9 N ( for the sake of this calculation I used a force of 700 N )

<img width="650" height="271" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d08a02b6-e5c3-49e3-8965-19b32dd3aef6" />


In terms of usability, this design allows for a nutcracker to be broken by the average human grip strength
Its design is also independent of the diameter of the nuts, meaning different types of nuts can be cracked with this appliance
In addition, a linear actuator can also be combined with this device, provided that its stroke is greater than 10 mm (which is very common) and that the force provided is greater than the average human grip strength of 700 N
